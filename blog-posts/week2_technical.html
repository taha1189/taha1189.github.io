What is the difference between Margin, Border, and Padding?

This box is a div; a container to put stuff in, in this case, text... This "div" has a solid gray border around it which is 5 pixels wide.

The space between this text and the border is known as padding. This div has padding of 20px all around.

Margins clear a space around an element. Margins are like padding, except they are the space from the edges of our divs starting at the border. This particular div has a margin of 300px from the left border to the left edge of the screen.
